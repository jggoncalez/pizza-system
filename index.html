About Us

Welcome to Good Old Pizza, where tradition meets flavor! Our story began in 1985, in a small corner of the city, with a passion for crafting the perfect pizza. Inspired by the classic recipes of the past, we set out to create a place where families and friends could gather to enjoy delicious, handmade pizzas in a cozy, retro atmosphere.

Over the years, Good Old Pizza has become a beloved spot for pizza enthusiasts. Our secret lies in our commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored techniques. From our signature dough, made fresh daily, to our rich tomato sauce and carefully selected toppings, every pizza is a labor of love.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while embracing the future. Whether you're here for a slice of nostalgia or to create new memories, Good Old Pizza is proud to serve you. Thank you for being part of our journey!
